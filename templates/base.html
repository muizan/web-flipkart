About us

Horus is flask app in which you can register any product and it will notify whenever this product fall below a a certain price .

why we made Horus?

sometimes we want to buy a product but its current price is too high so we add the product to our cart and keep on checking for its price to go down but it is a quite tiring process so Horus will automate this process and check the price of products at a certain interval of time.

what made Horus different?

There are a lot of website that do the same thing what make Horus different is that it does not moniter our browser or keep track of our shopping history.If we want Horus to keep track of some product you can just go to the website and register your product it will not moniter your browser.

Why we named it Horus?

Horus is an Egyptian god who eyes sees it all and it keeps track of activies of all the humans and our website also do the same thing it also keep track of everything that it is in its database.
